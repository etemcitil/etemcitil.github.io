ABOUT ME

Hello, I am Beatriz from Spain

I am an experienced Spanish teacher with over 11 years of dedicated teaching practice. Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of working with a diverse range of students, including adults, teenagers, and even corporate clients. My expertise spans from nurturing complete beginners with zero knowledge of Spanish to preparing advanced students for academic exams such as DELE, IGCSE, and IB programs, of which I also serve as an examiner. I take immense pride in the success stories of my students, as their achievements are a testament to our collaborative efforts. Currently residing in the vibrant city of Malaga, Spain, I am excited to extend my passion for teaching to new students, eager to embark on their own Spanish language journey. Together, we'll unlock the beauty of this language and culture, making your learning experience both enjoyable and rewarding.
